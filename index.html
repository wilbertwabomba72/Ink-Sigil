Ink Sigil

Trace glyphs. Cleanse living ink.

A focused paper-and-ink puzzle game about glyph order, clean strokes, and cleansing pressure.

About the Game

Ink Sigil is a focused single-player glyph puzzle game. Pick glyph cards, trace spell marks, and cleanse spreading ink before the seal breaks.

  • Paper-and-ink art direction inspired by calligraphy and old manuscript seals.
  • Simple touch play: choose glyphs, trace the mark, then cast.
  • Wave-based levels with rising pollution pressure and bright cleansing feedback.
  • Offline play, saved progress, level selection, music, and sound controls.
