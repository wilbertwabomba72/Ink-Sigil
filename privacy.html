Ink Sigil Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Ink Sigil Effective Date: June 20, 2026 Ink Sigil is a single-player casual puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the game. Contact Phone: +86 13612372765 Email: 13534266296@163.com Information We Do Not Collect Ink Sigil does not require an account. We do not ask for your name, address, contacts, photos, microphone access, or precise location. Gameplay progress is stored locally on your device. Advertising and Tracking The game may use third-party advertising SDKs to show ads. These partners may process technical information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, approximate region, app version, device model, operating system version, ad impressions, clicks, and diagnostic data. On iOS 14 and later, the game asks for App Tracking Transparency permission before accessing the advertising identifier for tracking. You can decline tracking and still play the game. If permission is denied, advertising may be less personalized. Device and Security Information The game may use device identifiers such as IDFV and security SDK checks to protect the app, prevent abuse, and support ad integrity. These checks are used for app security and fraud prevention. Third-Party Services Ink Sigil may integrate advertising and measurement services including Chartboost, Yandex, Vungle, and InMobi. These services process information under their own privacy policies. Children Ink Sigil is not designed to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it. Data Retention Local gameplay progress remains on your device unless you delete the app or reset its data. Advertising and diagnostic data retention is handled by the relevant third-party providers. Your Choices You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings. You can also limit ad personalization through your device privacy settings. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy when the app, SDKs, or legal requirements change. The updated policy will be posted with a new effective date.
